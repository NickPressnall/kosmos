KOSMOS

The simple KSP Mod Manager

$ kosmos install

Learn more »

Kosmos is a mod manager.

It downloads and installs mods for you.

Kosmos is awesome because ...

It keeps backups of your game

Any seasoned KSP modder will tell you that keeping copies of your KSP is very important. Without backups, uninstalling mods is impossible.

With Kosmos, you don't even need to worry about it. Kosmos will keep backups of your data for you.

It's human-curated

Unlike any other mod manager, Kosmos has humans describe how a package should be installed. This is why Kosmos so much "smarter" than any other mod manager.

Did you know that B9 Aerospace needs two extra fixpacks to work properly? With any other mod manager you would forget this, but with Kosmos, those two extra fixpacks are installed for you automatically.

It works on all platforms

Whether you're on PC, Mac, or Linux, Kosmos has you covered.

Kerbal Space Program works on all platforms. So should your mod manager.

Still not convinced?

Here's a quick demo of how powerful Kosmos is.

Scott Manley made a video showing how to install the mods for interstellar quest.

That video is 20 minutes long.

With Kosmos, you could install all of those mods in just a single command:

$ kosmos install active-texture-management b9 kerbal-alarm-clock kw-rocketry ksp-interstellar ferram deadly-reentry kethane infernal-robotics distant-object-enhancement better-atmospheres remote-tech-2 tac-life-support enhanced-navball kerbal-joint-reinforcement docking-port-alignment-indicator safe-chute kerbal-attachment-system real-chute tac-fuel-balancer
